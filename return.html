REFUND-AND-RETURN-POLICY

Returns

In the event, the Items delivered to you in any of the following conditions: Items that are physically damaged or defective; Items that varies from the description; Items that are wrongly delivered; Wrong Color; Wrong Style; Wrong Size; or Wrong Quantity; We will replace the items with new items of your choice in accordance with this Policy. If you have size or fitting issue, you can return the product within 7 days of delivery. For all returns, please email our customer care executive at info@estore.in along with your complete order details and issues. We will pick the product from your doorstep and ship you the required order once we receive the shipment. There will be no extra shipping charge levied by us. If you wish to change design, style or product, then you have to make a return Request and then, place a new order. We will accept Return of an Item only if the following conditions are met: Return should be initiated within 7 days of delivery of the Item; The tags and labels on the item should be intact; The Product should be unwashed, unused, and in undamaged condition; and The Product is returned along with the original packaging. Please be informed that the Returns are not acceptable for any request that do not meet the aforementioned criteria. Furthermore, please do not accept the items in case the product packaging has been tampered during the course of delivery.

Cancellation of orders:

You can cancel any orders before the product has been shipped. You can cancel your orders by sending an email along with your Order Id to info@estore.in from your registered email address. Our Customer Service Executives will contact you for confirmation of the cancellation request. Once the cancellation request has been made from your end, it will take us a maximum of 1-2 business days to cancel your order. After confirmation of the cancellation request, your entire order amount will be refunded to the bank account from which you had made the order within 4 to 7 days depending on the policy of your Bank. You will be notified about the cancellation of your order via email. We may, at any time at our sole discretion, cancel your order after intimating you about our decision to cancel your order by writing to your registered email id. In the event of any such cancellation, you may opt to replace the order with a new order after making any additional payment as applicable after adjustment or request for the refund of the amount to your bank account.

Refunds:

We do refunds only in genuine cases of delivery of defective products and on no other grounds. In the event of delivery of defective products, you have to send an email to our customer support executives at info@estore.in along with the details of the order, nature of issues, photographs of the defective product. In the event, on our verification, if it is observed that the defect is incurred due to any of your actions or omissions, then estore will not be responsible for refunding the amount paid by you. All applicable refunds shall be made to the bank account from which you had processed the payment. Please note the applicable shipping charges shall not be refunded to you in any circumstances.
